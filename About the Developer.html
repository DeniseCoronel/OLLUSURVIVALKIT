About the Developer

Hello, my name is Denise Coronel. I am very proud of this website and the true reason I created this website for many reasons. The main thing that motivated me was remembering when I was a freshman at Our Lady of the Lake. I was in my first class and my first professor said "Okay guys the assignments will be on blackboard," and I had no idea what she was talking about. Throughout my college career I have heard continously how students do not know how to access day-to-day resources at OLLU. I decided that I would create this website for those who are/were struggling like I was.
