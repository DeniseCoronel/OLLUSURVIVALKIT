How to access your Blackboard

Blackboard is another essential that I have seen OLLU students not know how to access. This element for the survival kit is so crucial. On this website Professor have basically all the course information, you can access your grades, etc.

Starting off on your OLLU portal once again. Hit the Blackboard link at the top. A second option is to scroll down and hit the Blackboard link. You will automatically be directed to your Blackboard page where as you can see, gives you access to many elements for success.
