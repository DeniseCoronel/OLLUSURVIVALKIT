How to access the OLLU ACE Center

I wanted to show students how to access the Our Lady of the Lake tutoring center or the ACE center because there is no better place to go when you simply need some help. However it is hard for them to take walk ins especially when they are helpng many students.

You will want to visit the link (www.ollusa.edu/studentsucesscenter) and scroll down and click the SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT. Use your OLLU Portal log in credentials to log in and make an appointment for Math, English or any other subject.
