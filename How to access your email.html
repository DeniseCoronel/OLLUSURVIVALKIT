How to access your OLLU E-MAIL

Since this an OLLU survival kit I wanted to include one of the most important elements for an Our Lady of the Lake student. The Our Lady of the Lake email is important because it gived you access to communicate OLLU professors, and peers.

Starting off by logging into your OLLU portal. (www.ollusa.edu/login). The email link is going to be towards the bottom of the page where I have highlighted. You will automatically be directed to this page. Select Outlook. This is what your email looks like. Now you can receive information and send information to anyone on campus. If you are struggling to find someone hit NEW, TO, and select the group OFFLINE GLOBAL ACCESS GROUP and everyone on campus will appear.
